Halo Health



For this project the client needed a complete brand work up for her new pharmacy consulting business; 'Halo Health'. This included logo design, brand color selection, font selection, social media branding, business card design and a full brand guidelines to ensure the brand was future proofed and would remain consistent if more design work was needed.

The logo mixes a classic font with the bold iconography of a haloed angel whose wings form the letters 'H H'.

A deep professional blue mixes with a hospital green to bring the two important elements of the businesses' nature to front of mind.


A horizontal version of the logo and a word mark were also provided to allow for use across a variety of platforms and contexts.


A neat mirrored business card rounded out the brand package and was provided along with a full brand guidelines and all working files.
